Controls

Gamepad

  • Movement: left analog stick or D-pad
  • Jump: A or Y button
  • Shoot: B or X button

Keyboard (Player 1, Player 2)

  • Movement: WASD, IJKL
  • Jump: E, O
  • Shoot: R, P
  • Fullscreen: F
  • Mute: M

Powerups

  • Feather: increased mobility/reduced gravity
  • Rocket boot: unlimited jumps
  • Slingshot: unlimited shots
