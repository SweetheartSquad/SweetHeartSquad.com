A game about driving around a mercenary in your taxi.

Use A and D or Left and Right to advance dialogue and make choices. Left is yes, right is no.

Entries in the series: Taxi Quest 64 | Taxi Quest 65 | Taxi Quest 66 | Taxi Quest 67

Made by IAN with help from Sean for Ludum Dare 34. The original jam build was made with S-Tengine2, and the web version was made with PixiJS.

Music: Suave Standpipe - Kevin MacLeod

Content warnings Discussion of death, loss, disaster.
