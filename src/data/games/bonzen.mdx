Nothing grows in space, at least not with some help. Join an old
  man in his quest to bring back beautiful greenery that once covered his lost
  planet. Take a step back from space drama and a step into his biodome to
  cultivate tiny bonsai trees and find some inner peace.
---
Walk up to pots with WASD to begin trimming.

Click and hold the right-mouse button to rotate around the trees and left-mouse button to trim leaves in order to shape your tree.
