<blockquote style="">Somewhere outside a small village, nestled deep in the wood...<br style="">
An immortal dragon makes its den.<br style="">
The village calls on heroes: Noble, strong, and good...<br style="">
But they fall again and again.</blockquote>
<p style="">A cute but deadly deckbuilding roguelike with unique spark. Carefully strategize as you make your way through the dragon's dungeon, and save the village from calamity!</p>
<ul style="">
<li style=""><strong style="">Build and burn your deck:</strong> Every card is precious, but some things are more precious than cards...</li>
<li style=""><strong style="">Battle your enemies:</strong> Overcome dozens of turn-based encounters and unique bosses.</li>
<li style=""><strong style="">Bring your party to victory:</strong> Learn to use each character's strengths to make it to the next camp; if your party falls, they'll have to start again!</li></ul>
<blockquote style="">Adventures are just around the corner, so make sure your heroes are well rested...</blockquote>
<hr style="">
<p style="">Controls: Mouse / Arrows + Enter / Gamepad</p>
<p style="">Mobile controls: Tap cards to begin, swipe left and right to pick a card, swipe up to play a card</p>
