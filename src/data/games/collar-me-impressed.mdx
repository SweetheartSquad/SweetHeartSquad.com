- Click the arrows to change the cat, collar, and background.
- Click and drag the pieces on the left to decorate your collar.
- To rotate a piece, scroll the mousewheel while you're dragging the piece.
- To delete a piece, drag and release it at the edge of the screen.
- When you're done, click **Save** to download a picture of your cat.
