You are the bearer of the Big Soul. Many covet the Big Soul. Protect your Big Soul!

---

Controls:

- [Left Mouse Button] - Shoot Fire Ball
- [Right Mouse Button] - Shoot Water Ball
- [Both Mouse Buttons at Same Time] - Shoot Steam
- Holding down buttons will charge your shots!
- [WASD] - Movement
- [Left Shift] - Run
- [Spacebar] - Jump

Defeat the Hands! Defeat the Wizard!  
Fullscreen recommended.
