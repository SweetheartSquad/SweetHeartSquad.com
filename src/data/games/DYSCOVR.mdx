<p>Welcome to the DYSCOVR open beta for project LD36 on exo-planet 543.14.543T!</p>
<p> You (and your fellow DYSCOVRites!) will traverse the planet in your very own DYSCOVR ROVR, digging for exciting artifacts of a lost civilization.</p>
<p> When you find an artifact, give it a description using this DYSCOVR console. If you're not sure what to say about it, then you can see what other people wrote about the same artifact.</p>
<p> If you're not sure where to begin, press the help button on your console!</p>
