<h1 style="text-align: center;">Controls</h1>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">Gamepad</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;">
	Movement: left analog stick or D-pad
	<br>
	Jump: A or Y button
	<br>
	Shoot: B or X button
</p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">Keyboard (Player 1, Player 2)</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;">
	Movement: WASD, IJKL
	<br>
	Jump: E, O
	<br>
	Shoot: R, P
	<br>
	<br>
	Fullscreen: F
	<br>
	Mute: M
</p>
<h1 style="text-align: center;">Powerups</h1>
<p style="text-align: center;">Feather: increased mobility/reduced gravity<br>
Rocket boot: unlimited jumps<br>
Slingshot: unlimited shots
</p>
